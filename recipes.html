5 WAYS TO LIVE A MORE
SUSTAINABLE LIFE

1. Pee in the shower
Yes, it sounds gross, but it is SO good for the environment. Did you know that every time you pee in the shower, you save 6 liters of water?

2. Don’t leave the water running
A little water goes a long way. Turning off the water while brushing your teeth, lathering soap on your hands or shampooing your hair makes a bigger impact than you think.

3. Always bring a reusable bag
In Norway, each person uses about 160(!) plastic bags every year. That’s 800 million in total. Every single time you choose to bring your own bag, is a win for the environment.

4. Shop vintage
The clothing industry is one of the worst climate enemies. Try buying some used clothes! It’s a win win – it’s good for the environment, and you’ll have more unique outfits than if you were to buy something brand new.

5. Make sure to eat your food before you have to throw it away
You can be amazed by what incredible recipes you can come up with just by using the random stuff you have in your fridge or cupboard!

LEFTOVER TACO ROLLS

Got some leftovers from the taco night? Try these easy taco rolls!

  • 7 dl all purpose flour (use wholewheat if you want them a bit healthier)
  • 3 dl lukewarm water
  • 1 packet of dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/2 box chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion (leftovers from the taco)
  • The rest of the taco meat
  • The rest of the vegetables (like corn and bell peppers)
  • 150g shredded cheese (leftovers from the taco)

Step 1: Mix the packet of dry yeast with the water, and let it sit until the dry yeast has dissolved

Step 2: Add flour, salt and oil - mix well

Step 3: Let the dough rest and rise somewhere warm for 30-60min, can be a hot bathroom floor or in a sink with warm water

Step 4: Set the oven to 225 celsius (425 fahrenheit)

Step 5: Roll out the dough (might need a bit more flour if sticky)

Step 6: Mix the tomatoes in a bowl together with some spices of own desire, like basil and garlic, and pour it onto the dough

Step 7: Add the leftover taco meat and vegetables

Step 8: Add the cheese

Step 9: Roll the dough together, and cut into equally large rolls

Step 10: Bake for 15-18min, until the cheese has completely melted and the rolls are golden

CARROT SOUP

  • This is a delicious and healthy soup you can make from leftover ingredients!
    You can also replace some of the ingredients with something you need to finish!

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons ginger
  • 1 chili
  • 4 carrots
  • 5 dl water
  • 1 package vegetable broth
  • 1/2 container chickpeas
  • 1 dl cream
  • 1/2 lime
  • 1/3 fresh coriander

  • Chop onion, garlic, ginger and carrots

  • Fry these vegetables in a casserole with some oil or butter until the onion is shiny

  • Add the vegetable broth and water, and boil till the carrots are soft

  • Add chickpeas and cream, and use a stick blender to mash the soup

  • Add limes, coriander, salt and pepper to taste

  • Serve with some garlic bread!
