CLIMATE CHANGE
ONE OF THE LARGEST CHALLENGES OF OUR TIME?

We believe that a sustainable planet is not a distant utopia. Through action and making sustainable choices easier, we can all contribute to making this future a near reality. Each year we throw food that could feed the population of USA times 10. If we cut this in half by 2050 we can spare the planet from 87 gigatons of carbon dioxide. Read more about our solution here.

WE HAVE THE SOLUTION

We created an app that connects to your refrigerator using a sensor that is easy to install.

This combines old and new technology - your old smartphone and refrigerator, and a brand new sensor.

The app will tell you the contents of your fridge, and has an award system for being generous.

Read more about the app here.

WHAT IS FOMOFOOD?

FOMO - fear of missing out.

Our goal is to prevent your food from
missing out - it should be eaten
before it goes bad, and therefore has to be thrown away.

If you're not able to finish everything
in your fridge before its expiration
date, the best solution is for someone
else to use it.

DID YOU KNOW...

...that it's actually pretty easy to live a sustainable life?

Every day, we make choices.
And every day, we can make our choices more sustainable.

In addition to our app, we have made a list of
5 ways to live a more sustainable life
to inspire you to make more sustainable choices in your every day life.

We've also added some great recipes,
encouraging you to use your left overs,
and to make more meat free meals.

Our solution is based on UN's 13th
sustainable development goal
